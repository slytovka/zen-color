Welcome to Zen Color System

We use color prefernce to show how each person prioritizes their life. Color preference is innate. A preffered color, for example, visually inspires a person to feel their hopes and aspirations. A non-preferred color irritatingly reminds them of what is missing in their life.

About Zen Color

Zen Color System is based on the concept that color can be used to reveal the core of an individual's personality. Concept-based theories have given the world its most far-reaching inventions. Our system allows you to learn about yourself without feeling the pain of personality invasive questions. Don't let the fact that it's so easy discount the truth of this new approach.

There are two ways we access knowledge - via concepts and experimentation. With the help pf leading academic scholars, I put my concept to a rigorous test with well over 5,000 color profiles.

In a clinical comparison with America's leading personality evaluation systems, the Myers Briggs, the 16PF, and the Strong Interest Inventory, the following results were indicated.

  • 72% of participants believed that Zen Color System give them a stronger awareness of themselves
  • Over 80% of participants believed that Zen Color System better escribed how they live their lives.
